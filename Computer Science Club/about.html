About Us

The Chaparral Computer Science Club was founded January 15, 2020. We strive to teach students computer scienece through iOS development, coding workshops, collaboration projects, website development, and more. Meetings and Workshops will be during lunch and are not mandatory. During summer break, the club may offer a coding class to students of all ages. If interested in this oppertunity please contact Matthew Salaway.
